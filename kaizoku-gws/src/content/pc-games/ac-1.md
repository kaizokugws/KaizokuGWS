## About

Assassin's Creed is an action-adventure game. Experience Altaïr's journey as the first Assassin's Creed, set in 12th century Masyaf during the Third Crusade.

## System Requirements

- OS: Windows XP/Windows Vista (32/64-bit)
- Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo 2.6 GHz or AMD Athlon 64 X2 3800+
- Memory: 1 GB RAM (XP) / 2 GB RAM (Vista)
- Graphics: 256 MB DirectX 10 compatible card (NVIDIA GeForce 6800+ / ATI Radeon X1600+)
- DirectX: Version 9 or 10
- Storage: 8 GB available space
