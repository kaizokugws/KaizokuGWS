## About

Marvel's Spider-Man: Miles Morales follows the explosive rise of Miles Morales as he masters incredible powers that set him apart from his mentor, Peter Parker. When a fierce power struggle threatens to destroy his home, the aspiring hero must embrace the mantle of Spider-Man to save a Marvel New York. Discover the explosive powers of Miles' unique bio-electric Venom attacks and his mastery of camouflage as he takes on the Roxxon Energy Corporation and a high-tech private army in this standalone adventure.
