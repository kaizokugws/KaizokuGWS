## About

The Amazing Spider-Man is an open-world action-adventure game inspired by the 2012 film of the same name. Players control Peter Parker as Spider-Man, swinging through the streets of New York City and engaging in combat with various villains. The game features a new story that runs parallel to the events of the movie, introducing an original cross-species virus conspiracy plot. Players can use Spider-Man's web-slinging abilities for traversal, combat, and puzzle-solving.
