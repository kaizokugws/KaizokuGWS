## About

Dark Souls II: Scholar of the First Sin brings the franchise's renowned difficulty and deep gameplay to new heights. This definitive edition includes all previously released DLC content: Crown of the Sunken King, Crown of the Old Iron King, and Crown of the Ivory King. Journey to the cursed land of Drangleic as the Undead and discover the truth behind the curse. With reworked enemy placements, enhanced visuals, and expanded online multiplayer support for up to 6 players, Scholar of the First Sin is the ultimate Dark Souls II experience.
