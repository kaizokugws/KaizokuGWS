## About

Metro 2033 Redux is a post-apocalyptic first-person shooter set in the ruins of Moscow, where survivors fight for survival in the subway tunnels after a nuclear war devastates the surface.

- Processor: Dual Core CPU (2.2+ GHz Dual Core CPU or better)
