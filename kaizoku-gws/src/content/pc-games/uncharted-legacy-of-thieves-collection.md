## About

UNCHARTED: Legacy of Thieves Collection includes the critically acclaimed single-player experiences from UNCHARTED 4: A Thief's End and UNCHARTED: The Lost Legacy. Play as Nathan Drake and team up with veteran treasure hunter Victor Sullivan on a globetrotting adventure to discover the legendary lost treasure of pirate Captain Henry Avery. In The Lost Legacy, play as Chloe Frazer who must team up with mercenary Nadine Ross in a race to find the Tusk of Ganesh, an artifact that could save her family, in the mountains of India.
