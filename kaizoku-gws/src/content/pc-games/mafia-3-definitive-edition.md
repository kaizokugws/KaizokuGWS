## About

Mafia III: Definitive Edition is a remake of the classic Mafia III game. Set in 1968, the game follows Lincoln Clay, a Vietnam veteran who returns home to find his adopted family murdered by the Italian mafia. Seeking revenge, Lincoln builds a new criminal empire and systematically dismantles the mafia's grip on the city of New Bordeaux. The game features an expansive open world set in a reimagined New Orleans.
