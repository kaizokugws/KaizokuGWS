## About

Elden Ring: Nightreign is a standalone co-op experience set in the world of Elden Ring. Up to three players team up to survive increasingly dangerous nights in the ever-shifting realm of Limveld. By day, explore the land, gather resources, and grow stronger. By night, face relentless waves of increasingly difficult enemies. With unique characters, each with their own abilities and playstyles, Nightreign delivers a fresh take on the FromSoftware formula focused on cooperative survival gameplay.
