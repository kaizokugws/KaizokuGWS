## About

Detroit: Become Human is an interactive drama action-adventure game developed by Quantic Dream. The story follows three androids: Kara, who escapes her owner to explore her newfound sentience; Connor, whose job is to hunt down deviants; and Markus, who is dedicated to freeing his fellow androids.

Set in Detroit during the year 2038, androids serve humans in everyday life. The game features a branching narrative with multiple endings based on the player's decisions.
