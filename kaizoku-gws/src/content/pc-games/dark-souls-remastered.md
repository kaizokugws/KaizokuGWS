## About

Dark Souls: Remastered includes the main game plus the Artorias of the Abyss DLC. Then, there's Lordran. A land of ancient lords, where the undead are cursed with the Darksign. Explore a dark fantasy world filled with interconnected areas, fearsome bosses, and deep lore. This remastered version features improved frame rate, resolution up to 4K, and enhanced visual effects while preserving the punishing difficulty and rewarding gameplay that defined the original.
