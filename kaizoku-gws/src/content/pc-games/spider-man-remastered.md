## About

Marvel's Spider-Man Remastered brings the critically acclaimed PS4 exclusive to PC. Swing through a vibrant Marvel's New York City as Peter Parker, an experienced crime-fighter with eight years of being Spider-Man under his belt. When a ruthless crime lord known as Mr. Negative unleashes a new wave of crime that threatens the city, Spider-Man must balance his life as Peter Parker with his responsibilities as Spider-Man. This remastered version includes all three DLC chapters: The Heist, Turf Wars, and Silver Lining.
