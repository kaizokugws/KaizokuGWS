## About

God of War Ragnarök is a third-person action-adventure video game developed by Santa Monica Studio and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It is the sequel to the 2018 God of War game and the ninth installment in the God of War series.

The game continues the story of Kratos and his now teenage son Atreus as they face the impending Ragnarök, the destruction of the Norse world. Kratos must battle gods and monsters alike while protecting his son from a prophesied death.
