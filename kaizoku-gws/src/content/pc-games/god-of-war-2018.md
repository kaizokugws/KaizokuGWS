## About

God of War is a third-person action-adventure video game developed by Santa Monica Studio and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It is the eighth installment in the God of War series, the eighth chronologically, and a sequel to 2010's God of War III.

The game follows Kratos, the former Greek God of War, who is now living in the world of Norse mythology with his young son Atreus. The story is a reimagining of the franchise, with a focus on Kratos' relationship with his son as they journey to fulfill a personal mission.
